About Mission

ABOUT COLLEGE

Srinivas Institute of Technology (SIT) is one of the premier engineering colleges in the Mangaluru region, committed to delivering high standards of education through a holistic and student-centric approach. Recognized by AICTE and affiliated to VTU, SIT provides a vibrant academic environment hosting over 3,000 students. Our campus fosters innovation and excellence, empowering students to become future leaders in technology.

  • SIT emphasizes industry-oriented learning through hands-on projects, research initiatives, and skill development programs.
  • The institution maintains strong academic-industry collaboration to enhance real-world exposure.
  • A culture of discipline, innovation, and leadership defines the SIT learning ecosystem.

ABOUT SOLVEATHON 1.0

Solveathon 1.0 is a 24-hour technology-driven event designed to foster innovation, creativity, and practical problem-solving among students. It serves as a platform where participants can transform ideas into functional solutions through coding and collaboration. Join us to challenge yourself, network with industry experts, and showcase your technical prowess on a grand stage.

  • Participants will work on real-world problem statements under time-bound conditions.
  • The event promotes teamwork, critical thinking, and rapid prototyping skills.
  • Solveathon 1.0 aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations.

Protocols

  • Open to all UG, PG, and Engineering students.
  • Category Segregation:
      • UG Category – evaluated separately
      • MCA/MSc/BE & Engineering Category – evaluated together
  • Teams must bring and assemble their own hardware on-site.
  • Use of open-source libraries and frameworks is allowed; however, pre-existing code, templates, or assets are strictly prohibited.
  • All development must be done only during the hackathon period.
  • Team size: 4 members.
  • Each team must submit a working prototype and deliver a PPT presentation.
  • Problem statements will be provided on the spot.
  • Plagiarism or rule violations will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
